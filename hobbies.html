My Hobbies

My present work is actually been my lifetime passion, with hopes of one day helping mankind escape from our cradle in the solar system and explore the cosmos. However, I do enjoy spending my time pursuing the following activities.

  • Reading

    I like reading books related to sci-fi adventures and murder mysteries. Asimov and Michael Crichton are among my favourites in the sci-fi categories. Sleuths like Sherlock Holmes, Hercule Poirot and Nancy Drew also inspire me.

  • Gardening

    I have always admired greenery and am an advocate for sustainability. This has stemmed from my deep connection with nature. I pride myself on having a green thumb. I plan to have a bonsai tree as my family heirloom.

  • Music

    A newly acquired hobby of mine, I am learning the guitar and hope to start a band someday. Wish me luck!
